Description:

This is a recipe for a yummy melt in your baked sweet Potato. it is quick to cook and will taste good with any main dish.

Ingredients

  • 4 sweet potatoes, scrubbed clean
  • 4 tbsp. butter
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
