Steak

Description:

This is a recipe for a yummy melt in your mouth steak. it is quick to cook and will taste good with any side.

Ingredients

  • 1 steak, your choice
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Let steak sit at room temperature for 30 minutes
    2. .
  2. Preheat oven to broil and position rack closest to heating element. On a baking sheet, rub steak with 1 tablespoon oil then season generously with salt. Season with pepper if desired.
  3. Broil steak about 10 to 12 minutes, flipping halfway through. Internal temperature of steak should be 125° for rare, 135° for medium rare, and 145° for medium.
  4. Let steak rest for 10 minutes before slicing, against the grain.
